Dr J.G.M. Ramsey, Knoxville, Tennessee

Page 2

Nashville, Novr. 13th, 1879. Dr JGM Ramsey, Knoxville, My dear sir: It is my duty to appoint a delegate to represent the State of Tennessee in the Yorktown Centennial Celebration. If you will accept the appointment I will confer it upon you as it belongs to you as a matter of right. Advise me at you earliest convenience whether you you accept and oblige Your friend Albert S. Marks
