Charlotte N.C Wednesday Jany 4th 1871 Dr J. G. M. Ramsey at Mr W.D. Alexander's Dear Sir I reached here this morning after a short trip to Oakland & will remain duing the day. Mc and myself have made a partial settlement & I think if I could see you, we could easily complete it. You will grealy oblige me by coming to Charlotte this morning, as I am obliged to leave to-morrow. You will find me at the house of Hon. I. H. Wilson Yours truly & respectfilly Geo. T. Barnes
