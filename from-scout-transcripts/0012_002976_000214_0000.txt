Department of State Washington , Aug. 29, 1845 John Pedrick Esq. New York . Sir. Agreeably to the request, contained in your letter of the 23d inst, I herewith enclose the Commission of your Son, John C. Pedrick Esq. late Consul of the United States at Rio Grande , & which he inadvertently enclosed in the letter tendering his resignation. Of the receipt of his letter, and the acceptance of his resignation, he has been duly notified. I am, Sir, respectfully Your obt. Servant. James Buchanan
