Nashville Tenn Apl 26 My Dear Boys I thought this afternoon that I would sit down and write you a few lines I wrote James a few lines last Monday in his Mother s Letter I write too you often but you do not them somehow James wants me to keep his Letters I shall Send them Back him so he can take care of them James when you write again i wish you would write a little about Andrew Thos Sharon and the Babe I think that Andrew could write a few lines along with you you do not say whether you had your Borders made or not nor if there any sign of grapes yet I have seen some here last week and the trees are loadened down with Mullberries Tell me when you wrote to Rosa Baily and give me all the news that you know and try and be good boys and do what your Mother tells you

you and andrew must not quarrel now you are big enough to know Better now tell me something about the weather with you it is getting Pretty warm here now it is cold morning and Greniy ' s through Charley Magee is sick and at the Hospital I will send you a paper along with this Letter I have nothing else to tell you at this time next time i write i may have Some more news to write to you I am your Father David My Dear Wife Since i answered your letter on last Monday I have nothing Particular to write to you no more than i am well hopeing that you and the children are well too Chaley Magee is sick and is gone to the Hospital i think that he is Consumptive Mr Carnel came here last Sabbath Just as i got your letter finished he is working at Nashville yet but he thinks that he is going to Chatanooga but i think that they will discharge good many of them John Carnel is well give my respects to Jane Mr & Mrs White I am yours truly David
