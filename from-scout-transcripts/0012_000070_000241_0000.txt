Knoxville Oct 8 1863 Hon E. M. Stanton Secretary of War Washington, D.C. Sir I have the honor to introduce to you Col O.P. Temple a resident of East Tennessee and a man thoroughly identified with the people of this portion of the state - He is a gentle man in whom I have the utmost confidens as to integrity of charactir & purpose, and his statements you may fuly rely upon. He is a strong friend of the Union, and will be able to give you much valuable information as to the condition of this people their loyalty, the raising of troops etc. I command him to your very kind attention I have the honor to be Very Respectfuly Your Obt Svt A E Burnside Maj Genl
