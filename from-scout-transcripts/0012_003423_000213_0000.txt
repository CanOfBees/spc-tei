Camp Near Chattanooga Sept 25 Dear Sister It has been Some tim Since I heard frome you I have Some bad news to wright you this time we fought the Enemy on the 19th & 20th of this month and drove him 12 miles in doing So we lost a great many Brave men your Husband was one of them he fell on Saturday the 19th Bravely Bateling for his home. The old 51st lost very heavy. The Company that I am in lost 15 wounded none killed our Genl was killed on the night of the 19th. Me an Harvey Starnes are safe so far but do not know how long we will be so we are within 2 miles of the Enemy and expect to fight every hour nothing more Your Brotre H Parson
