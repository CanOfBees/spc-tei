Private Nashville Tenn. Octbr 23rd 1861 Col O.P. Temple My Dear Sir I have just learned from Judge Briant, this morning that in addition to Brownlow -- Col Trigg, Col Jso Williams and yourself, are to be indeted in the federal court, here, this week for Treason. I have barely time to write this much Yours Truly R.H. Hodsden Judge Brian told me about it in confidence. Thornburg will be released to day. R.H.H.
