Charles Pettit Esqr. President of the Insurance Co of North America Geo. Meade

Page 2

Dr. Sir I wish to know if the Office have come to a determination respecting the Insurance made on the Columbia Capt. Franklin, I wish to have the present Policy Cancelled &a new one open &as the illegible May be heavily looked for I wish not to have this business delayed. I am with esteem &respect Dr Sir Your Most Hble Servt. June 18th 1796 Geo. Meade Charles Pettit Esqre
