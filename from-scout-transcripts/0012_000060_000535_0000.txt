Nashville, Jan 13, 1886, Mrs. J.G.M. Ramsey The Tennessee Historical Society takes pleasure in acknowledging the receipt from you of Certificate of indebtedness, C.S.A. for $1000, issued Aug. 1, 1862, at Richmond, Va. as a gift to said society. You will please accept their thanks for this expression of your interest in the objects of their Institution. Very respectfully, J. A. Cartwright
