Camp at Murfreesb Tenn June 5th 1863 Brother Crit I sent my seat my self this evening to rite you a few lines to let you know that we are all well at presant. the weather is very warm and rainey. we have marching orders now for some place I doent know where we have Eight days rashions packed now. we have five in our napsack and three in our haversack. There were an man
