NashvilleTenn July 17th/65 My Dear Wife Just after coming off guard and after taking a little dinner I take the pleasure of writing you a few lines in hope that they will find you all well as they leave me through Mercy I have not much time to write for i have to go on at four o clock again there is great Preperations here to day they expect to have a general Inspection of everything in our Regt to day but i am afraid that it wont take place for it is raining very pleasant Just now There was a Party or spree whatever they call it close to our Barracks i was standing guard all night handy the house so that i could hear the dancing so it appears that all our Officers got an invitation to it they had all kinds of drinks there and it broke up in a fight so our Officers is keeping it up too day I was talking with our first Lieut this morning and he was awful drunk after that i saw the Cap and him and a good many more at the Sutter s shanty this morning he has nothing to sell but Ale and Whisky so I asked a man dinnter time if Wiley was

Drunk he said he was pretty drunk all the morning I think there must be something up do not tell any one what i write you If we had been doing the same duty that we did when we first came we would have been home for that is Played out And the day that we now do there is not men enough to do it and getting less every week between siskness and one thing or other there was two fellows left for parts unknown last night they rode off on a mule apiece. They are Paying off soldiers every day There is an awful sight of soldiers around here yet for all that The report is that there is another Regt here for to take our Place i hope it may be true Now I have nothing Perticular to write I have mentioned in two or three letters that i have Recieved two letters from you with two Dollars in each I would not send any more in a letter until i shall ask you I want to take this to the Post myself for some would destroy the Letter for the sake of the stamp Please Kiss All the Children for me give my Respects to Mr & Mrs White I have not heard from Jimy yet I remain yours David
