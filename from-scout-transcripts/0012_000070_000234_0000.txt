Tallapoosa Ga Nov 23rd 1861 Temple & Rogers gents will you do me the kindness to inform me what has been done with Mr. Brownlow I fill a grate anxiety in hearing what has become of him for many such men as he is has never marked the face of this globe would to God that there was one thousand in Georgia that was as unyielding as he hoping that he may long survive this struggle and once more be the participant of a Renowned Republican Government one that suits "him." I Remain yours Very Respectfully T Sampford Garner PS Please answer me soon TSG
