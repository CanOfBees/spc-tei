New York 5th July 1793 Sir Above you have an order on the Bank of the United States for One hundred Dollars being the payment that will be Cui the 8th. Instant on One hundred Shares wch. I hold in the Insurance Company of North America I am with respect Sir Your obe Servt. Dom Lynch Ebenezer Hazard Esqr.
