At a meeting of the Trustees of the 2.d Presbyterian Church April 5. 1838. Resolved That a Committee of three persons be appointed to negotiate for a loan or loans to this Church on mortgage of its real Estate in third above Arch Street or any part thereof, of Such sums as they shall judge proper for liquidating in whole or in part the outstanding debts for the construction and furniture of the new Church_ And that the President and Secretary be authorized and directed to affix the Corporate Seal to such bonds and mortgages as shall be presented to them by that Committee for execution in the name of the Church. Mrs Chauncey Macalister &Kane were appointed that Committee_________ Extract from the minutes Sam Hazard Secy
