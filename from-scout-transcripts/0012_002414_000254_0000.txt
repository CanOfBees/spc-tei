James Latimer Esq. G.M. Dallas Septr 12, 1822

Page 2

Dear Sir, Permit me to request your attention to the foregoing draught of a deposition, which I have prepared at the direction of Wm. Meades. Alter it in any way you like _ make it precisely conformable to your recollection of the facts _ if, by any detail, you can strengthen the main point, I will thank you to do so:- And be good enough to let me have it again, for final preparation, as soon as you conveniently can. I am, very respy. &truly D. Sio , Yrs. G. M. Dallas. 12. Sept. 1822. James Latimer Esq.
