Salem, N.C., Mar. 23rd 1893 Dr. J.G.M. Ramsey Dear Sir: - If within your power will you kindly fill out the following blank concerning Margaret B. Crozier, who was formerly a pupil of Salem Female Academy, and return it to me? My object is to obtain this short record of the subsequent life of each girl who has been a pupil within that widely known and honored Institution. Very Respectfully, Adelaide L. Fries
