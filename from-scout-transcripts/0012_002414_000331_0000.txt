Letter Secry of the Treasury US. Feb y. 21. 1798 Relating to Piers in River Delaware. Thomas Fitzsimons Esquire Philadelphia

Page 2

Treasury Department February 21.st 1798 Sir, The memorial of the Merchants of Philadelphia praying for the erection of certain Piers having been refered to me, I have to request that you would take measures for obtaining a specification of the proposed improvements, and an estimate of the experience; with such other information as will enable me to place the subject properly before the House of Representatives. _ I am very respectfully Sir, your mo: obedt Servant. Oliver Wolcott Thomas Fitzsimons Esqr
