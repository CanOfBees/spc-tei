Camp Near Lick Creek Bridge Nov 12 1863 Dear Wife I have the pleasure this Eavening of Seating My Self to drop You a few Lins I heard Something this Morning and I was glad to hear that our forces had got after Bill Nelson all that I am a frade of is tha they wont get him Andy Winters come to our camps this morning and Said that they was fighting about Parrotsville I wish I had bin there So I Could have got to Shoot at that hsavlsh Black hearted Bill Nelson That Night I left I went to Yenipling Lins and him and Lincoln sonh merit walk me to rersbng I have bin well Sence toea

With the Exception of the look hnkie but I am better If It to days I went to the Other day to worrying with the trains after conn I made old ng Karl & Kill Jc ngs & nall I taken live loads of Corn Kinser is threads and of Office I expect the Col. told Alexander that I Should be Captan, of the Company & Alex Will be first Lt. I heard to day our forces had taken a Grate Many prisners below and that they had got possession of Look Out Mountain , that is Close to to Chatanooga Chattanooga . I heard to day that the pay master was on the road here. I am afrade the Rebels has taken all I have and all youre father has. Andy Winters Sertha

I want to send You one to read and you can afet the histry of the Rebles Emms I am going to Send you Some Coffee & I will Send You some Sugar Some other time I think I No things happens.
