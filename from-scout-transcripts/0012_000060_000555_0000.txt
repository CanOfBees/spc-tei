Weatherford, Texas Nov. 17th 1902 Mrs. Lizzie Breck Knoxville Tenn Dear Madam - I hope you will not think me presumptuous in troubling you again about my questions along the Alexander line. You will doubtless remember my visit to you

Page 2

in regard to John McKnitt Alexander; - You spoke of the family bible record I am very desirous of a copy of said record remembering how smart and kind you were to me I make bold my request for a copy as it will be of great value to me the man I

read and know of your illustrious father the man I wish to know. Hoping you are enjoying excellent health, and thanking you in advance for said information I am Very Truly Yours Mrs. Oscar Barthold #802 Waco St Weatherford, Texas
