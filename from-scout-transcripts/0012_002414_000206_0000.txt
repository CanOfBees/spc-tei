5th Month May 18, 1809 John Dickinson Acknowledging Rect of Certificate of $2,000 at his Credit in Bank North America PAID Charles Petit Esquire Philadelphia paid

Page 2

My respected Friend, My Letter of the Second insland, with a copy of a certificate that I have credit in the Bank of North America for two thousand Dollars deposited by the Insurance Company of North America, is received and I thank thee for thy promptness and kind attentions to the Business on which We have lately corresponded. I am very sincerely thy friend, Jony Dickinson Wilmington The 18th of the 5 Month 1804 Charles Petit Esquire Philadelphia
