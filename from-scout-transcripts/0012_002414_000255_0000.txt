Messrs Price &Morgan Gentlemen From the conversation had with your Mr Morgan to day I offer you the Brig Globe to proceed from this to Her next &take on board the Cargo of Your Brig Hercules, &carry it to New Orleans for $1500 day Fifteen Hundred Dollars, allowing at Key West 6 lay days for loading, the Cargo to be delivered within reach of the vessels tackle, at Key West. You are aware the Globe is well calculated for the Voyage &is in every respect in first rate order, &can proceed forthwith on the Voyage. Yours very Respectfully John Devereux Phila August 2nd 1827
