Charles Pettit Esquire 2o Sheet Joseph Hopkinson 20 June 1804 Sir M Racole informs me that his Clients E. Watson &Co of New York are desirous of transfering then Suit against your Company to the Circuit Court of the U. States.__ Our consent to this measure is rerquested, as you will see by the enclosed note. Will you be pleased to say what answer I shall give to the proposal.__ A Trial may be expected more speedily in the U. S. Court that in the State Court, and I do not know that it is our interest to make delay _ With Respect Y. illegible Obed Serv Jos. Hopkinson June 20, 1804 Ch. Pettit Esqr
