Binghamton March 25 my dear son i now answer your kind letter that came to us on March 23 and we was glad to her you that you was enjoying a good state of health as it leaves us all well at this time bless god for so great a blessing my dear boy i was very glad to here from you and your dear brother also was glad and i feel very comfortable about you as i know Mr Curtis will be so kind to you if you are but a good boy as i beg you will be that you have meet with a kind friend that is Mr Curtis and i hope you will do all that is in your power to mind and obey him and then the lord bless you for it my dear boy you are young mind and do not be led in temptations for your young

mind may be easeliey easily led astray but i hope you will shun temptation and follow the right path to glorey my dear boy if you have any leasure time dont forget to read your bible that you taken with you i pray for you as a mother for the welfare of her darling child you are in my thoughts aleays because you are so young to be in a strange land without a parent with you but i hope not without a friend your dear father is a long way from you i supose he is in chattanooga i received a letter from him the night that Austin received yours he was not very well he had a bad cold upon he says he is not received any letter from me yet and i have sent two i whish you would look after his letters and forward them to him if you find any in the post offeces send them

to him right away as i sent the last to Nashville William smith chattanooga care his direction s c Meares he snet on thirty dolars and i received it safe and i sent him word and he is not receved my letter you must let him know if you can that we are all well send me men how you have your pay by the month or quarter or how if by the month my dear boy you can send me a little if you live as Austin we have a enought to do to make both ends meet he is hinden concsetreble with the blood bath breghes ar gone and the water in the two syanyy have meet and the water is A foot deep in the road nobody can pass ring had to move ever place is some out i have sent the accounts of in this peceis of News paper Austin got me to rite for him and he send his kind love to you and

he was so glad to here hear from you and he says when you come home he will have bully good times together he is saved you his gun and he says if father comes home before you he will come down to you if you get him a birth with you he says he should like to come down to Nashville litte Henery send you 60 thousand kises to his dear attey and thousand to his father give our best respects to Mr Curtis and Mrs Curtis uncle and aunt sent there kind love to you and there best respects to Mr and Mrs Curtis pat foley send his best respects to you and all the boy in the shop desire to be remember to you Austin best respects to fred Martin rite as often as you can and rite a good long letter Mrs Coleg and sue and daniel send there best respects to you So i must conclude with our love to you we remain your affectionate Mother and brother Eliza * Austin rite often dont forget Send these but of News to father if you can
