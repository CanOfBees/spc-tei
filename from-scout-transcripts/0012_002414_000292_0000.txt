Nov 17th 1794 Sir, Finding that it is impossible to procure the shares which by Contract we were respectively to have furnished, because they are not in the Market for sale we propose to settle those contracts by giving our Notes at 90 days from the times respectively at which the contracts became due, at 31 PCent Advance upon the shares _ The Notes to be indorsed by the other and drawn in the proportions as by each of the illegible Contracts _ we are sir with great respect your Obed Servants Robt Morris M. Nicholson John M. Nesbitt Esqr Presid of the north am. insurance Co.
