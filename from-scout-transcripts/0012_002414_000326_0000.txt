January 20. 1806 Willings &Francis Application for Loans on Respondentia &Ship Asia

Page 2

Sir We have an opporty. of securing some sources for our New Ship Asia, intended for Canton this Spring &we beg leave to propose a loan from your Office of 15 a 20000$. on the following Loans. We will give our rate pay able 1st. April next satisfactorily indorsed for the June loans with Estimates conditioned that on or before the 20th. March, you shall determine, whether you will let the Sum on supanderstia on the Asia or not. Should you decline the Loan, then it is understood that we shall pay our note, now given. We respectfully submit the above & illegible Your Obed Servt Willings &Francis. Pettit
