Knoxville. Tennessee Apl 15th. 1871. Dr J.G.M. Ramsey, Dear Sir, Our spring term of Chancery Court closed last Saturday. none of your Bank of E. Tenn. cases were taken up. A special term will commence on 5th Monday of next month, when it is probable they will be taken up. I will keep you advised. in great haste yours truly T. R.Carnick.
