Charles Pettit Esqre President of the Insurance Company of North America Jany 22 1802 Levi Hollingsworth &Son giving Notice of Policy in Sch. Samuel being Assigned to them

Page 2

Charles Pettit Esqre Sir We think it proper to inform the Insurance Company of North America that the Policy of the schooner Samuel; insured in your office, has been transferred to us. With much respect Your Obedt Svts Levi Hollingsworth &Son Philada Jany 22nd 1802
