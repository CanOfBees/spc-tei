Memo of contract with Mr. Phagan Store butler for one bread store. this contract made in the last summer or fall

Page 2

J.G.M. Ramsey I contracted with Mr. Phagan the store butler to make for my Grand-parents a bread store to be over their grocers in Lebanon Church yard & me to do the engraving as written by me & handed to him for which I am to pay him twenty five dollars ($25.00) on Christmas Dec. 25. 1881. J.G.M. Ramsey Oct. 31, 1881
