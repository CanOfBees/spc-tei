J Inskeep Esr Pres N A InCo Feb 1. 1812 George Harrisons' order to Pay Mr Willis his Dividend for Jany 1812

Sir Be pleased to pay the amount of Dividend due on the Stocks standing in my name to John Willis _ My Clerk, who presents you this _ &If necessary I will formalize this order when I get out bun illegible now Confined by in disposition _ YrobServ GeoHarrison Philad Feby. 1 . 1812 To John Inskeep Esqr Pres N A Insurance Co
