Col. Charles Pettit Sir Mrs. Copeland the Administrar. to Asa Copelands Estate, Has requested me to propare a Reference for the Settlement of her Accts. with The Insurance company of North America on your appointing a Referee and mentioning a time &place of meeting She will be ready on her part. She Judges this the most proper mode of Settlement to justify her as Adminir, Shall be obliged by your reply And am Respectfully Sir Your Obdt Servt._ Peter W. Gallandet 20 Decr 1799
