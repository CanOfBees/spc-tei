Know all men by these Presents: That I William Bingham of the City of Philadelphia req Do make constitute and appoint Denis Cottineau of Philadelphia to be my true and lawful Attorney for me _ and in my Name to Assign and transfer five hundred Shares of the Stock of the President and Directors of the Insurance Company of North America standing in my name _ with Power also an Attorney or Attorneys under him for that Purpose, to make and substitute; and to do all lawful Acts requisite for effecting the Premises; hereby ratifying and confirming all that my said Attorney or Substitute or Substitutes shall do therein by virtue hereof. In Witness whereof I have hereunton set my Hand and Seal the Twenty Ninth day of July in the Year of our Lord One Thousand Eight Hundred and one - Sealed and Delivered in the presence of Mr Bingham Alex Burning Thomas Wright lv Be it known, that on the twenty ninth Day of July One Thousand Eight Hundred and One _ before me Clement Brodle Esquire, Notary Public for the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, duly commissioned and authorised, dwelling in the City of Philadelphia, came William Bingham Esquire Above named _ and acknoledged the above Letter of Attorney to be per Act and Deed. In Testimony whereof, I have hereunto set my Hand and affixed my Notarial Seal the Day and Year last aforesaid. Clement Brodle NW. Perb
