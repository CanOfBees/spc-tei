Midway February 9th 1862 Mr O P Temple Knoxville, Tenn Dear sir we have not leart wether the prisoners that was convicted to be sent off from Knoxville are sent or not. you will please write to me when you get this and tell me wether my father has been sent off or not and if he has where he is sent too and when he started. I send you five cents to pay the postage on the letter you will write. yours Respectfully H.C. Smith
