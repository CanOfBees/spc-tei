Burlington February 16th 1819 John Inskip Esq Sir please pay to the order of Elihu Chauncy Esqr. the Dividends now due on my Stock in the North American Insurance office and illegible other Dividends as may hereafter become due, until this order is resolved by you __ Humble Servant Elias Boudinot
