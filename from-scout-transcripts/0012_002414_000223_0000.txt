Walter Bowne Power to Thomas Mc Euen

Page 2

KNOW all Men by these Presents, That I Walter Bowne of New York do make, constitute and appoint Thomas Mc Euen of Philadelphia my true and lawful Attorney irrevocable, for me and in my Name to sell assign and transfer to any Person or Persons Two hundred and thirty two shares of Stock standing in my Name in the Books of the Insurance Company of North America with Power also an Attorney or Attornies under him for that Purpose to make and substitute ; and to do all lawful Acts requisite for effecting the Premises ; hereby ratifying and confirming all that my said Attorney or his Substitute or Substitutes shall do therein by Virtue hereof. IN WITNESS whereof I have hereunto set my Hand and Seal the Twenty Fifth Day of January in the Year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and four Sealed and delivered in the Presence of Jno Keese Chas Reese Walter Bowne State of New York, sso BE IT KNOWN, That on the twenty fifth Day of January one thousand eight hundred and four before me, JOHN KEESE, Notary Public in and for the State of New-York, duly commissioned and sworn, came and appeared Walter Bowne within named, and acknowledged the above Letter of Attorney to be his Act and Deed. IN TESTIMONY whereof I have hereunto set my Hand, and affixed my Seal of Office, the Day and Year last aforesaid. Jno Keese Public Notary
