Ebenezer Hazard Esquire Secretary of Insur Coof North America Philadelphia Letter John Welsh Septr. 1. 1798.

Page 2

Christiana Bridge 1 Septr 1798 Sir Yours of 30 Ulto. is at hand_ I am directed to make Insurance on 10,000 dollars Goods &Ship Citizen Ezekiel Hubbell Master to Sail Next Sunday, or Soon after, at &from New York to Havana &at &from there back to New York_ She is armed with 14 Carriage Guns, Consisting of 4 &6 pounders &12 pound Caronades _ 5 Swivels _ Musquits Pistols &boarding pikes _ high waist boards formidably fitted_ Nettings ten feet high with a Chain upon the top from Stem to Stern_ 40 hands_ The property is owned by Americans &one of them goes out with it_ She is an American Ship _ will thank you to inform me at what rate it will be done? I shall have a Small Claim on your Office for Some things plundered by the Privateersman &out of Brig Active &the final Statement of the Porgas loss, I believe we must leave till we can return home again, but must request you to let me have on the 16th. of this month 3000 dolls. on the 23d.. 2000 dolls &on 28th. 3000 dolls more, or if you please, I will send the Policies of Porgas up, take the whole, or near it, &deduct the Interest for the time not-dasrd it will be due the 22 ins:_ I wish soon as I can get the money

to go to the Southward to purchase a Cargo for a vessel I have to Sail tomorrow_ Therefore it will be more agreeable to me to receive it Soon. I also wish to Know the premium on a good vessel from New Castle to Alexandria &from there to St. Kitts, or from Alexandria to St. Kitts loaded with Flour, American Cotton &property __ I am Sir Your ObedServ. John Welsh NB_ Having had agood deal to attend to in Wilmington, I observed for three days ending the 30th in evening, that 4 to 5 persons died each day & Mostly Inhabitants of that place_ One woman from Phila died at Christa Bridge, &only one Shal lop man in New Castle, tho Ccreiural are Cick in vessels lying in the road_ Itis extremely healthful here, &many places to See had, as very few families have Come So far___ provisions plenty & low. Welsh Ebenezer Hazard Esquire
