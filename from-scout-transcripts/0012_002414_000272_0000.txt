Sir There are probably some small dividend due on a few Shares of Stock in the Ins Co of North America held by me in trust for my children Henry, Thomas, and Mun , I shall therefore be obliged by your buying any that there are to my son Henry D Gilpin whom I name my Attorney for the purpose of receiving them as well now as in future. I remain very respectfully Sir. Your Humble Sert Jos: Gilpin April. 27. 1820. The President illegible Secretary of The Insurance Co of North America
