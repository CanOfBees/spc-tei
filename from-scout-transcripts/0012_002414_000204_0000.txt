President & Directors Insurance Compy N A Cornelius Comegys requesting renewal of a note -

Page 2

Phildadelphia 27 April 1802 Gentl. Aware of the inconvenience attending, applications of this kind & having some knowledge of your Funds it is with reluctance I have to ask the favor of a renewal of the Note given by Baker & Comegys for Insurance on the Brig Tartar which I flatter myself will be granted immedia a promise that I will not require further indulgence. I am very respectfully Your much obd sert. Cornl. Comegys President & Directors Insurance Compy N A
