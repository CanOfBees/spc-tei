Philada. Jany 8th. 1805 Gentlemen, In lieu of the Philada. Ins: Stock I have deposited in your office, Simon &Hyman Grates's obligation, (indorsed by Saml. Hays, myself and Willing's &Francis,) for the delivery of 20,000$ on the 1st of April next, and for which I request the release of Said Stock. _ I am Gentlemen respectfully your obedt. Servt. RWilling Jos: Tagart Messrs John Inskeep } Com: & Alex: Henry
