F. A. De Peyster New York Augt 3rd 1819 John R. Latimer Esqr Philadelphia

Page 2

Latimer Dear Sir I Rec yours the other day and was happy to hear of your arrival and health the one and will leave thy for Canton in all thy month. my letters you wish to forward to that place will take chage of with pleasure let them be here by 12th inst, Ontime 3d August. 1810 yours with Esteem F. A. De Peyster
