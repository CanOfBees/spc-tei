Greencastle Iowa January the 26, 1865 Mr O.P. Temple Dear Sir I notice your advertisement through the Knoxville Whig stating that you have 3,500 acors of land for sale in this stat law for cash or to swap for land in Tennessee & Mr Templ I am a tennessean by birth born and raised in Washington County, the fall so I emmigrated to Whitfield County Georgia ner Dalton I have a farm 6 Miles South of Dalton on the Consauga River containing 3.00 or 3.20 Acors the the last acount my Brouther Mosses was living on

Brother Moses at Danton Georgia an go down and see the farm and if we can be benefted by the exchang all right my farm was well improved when I left it in November 63 & if you will rite me stating whether your land is improved or not or what amount of timber you have I will give you the best traid I can & this climent dont sut me and I will return South as soon Has this infurnal Rebelian is put down and Sinect to Greencast Iowa Yours Jesse Glaze Jesse Glaze A Feb 6/65
