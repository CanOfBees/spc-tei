Sunday afternoon June 11 1865 Nashville my wife I write these lines hopeing they will find you all well at this time I thank god it leasves me pretty will at this time although my trouble is graite my dear wife I wrote these few lines to telly ou aboute my alfred he is very dangers sick in the ospetle now he whent to the hospital the 3 day of June being wheler wrote a letter o me on the 3 of June and I did not get it untill the eighte of june and he stated me in a letter the I must come to nashvill in aste

for alfred have got the tipefaver very bad and I must in haste for the docter said he coldnot live and the letter come to chattanooga to me I was gone 16 miles belo dalton I come to chattanooga on friday afternoon and a rived at nasvhill on saturday night my dar wife and I whent to the hospetle and I founde my dear alfred very sick with the fever and the diarea he did not know me then intell the next morning is is not senseble now he dont know only now and then my d. weife he canot speke much out at all that I can understant him

Page 3

he trys very harde to spoke to me and he cannot my dea wife this is trouble to me now I wish he ade come here a month ago henry wheeler said that is been sick this last month and he told him he had bette go home then before he got the fever I ham with him all the time day and night I keep ill cloths on is head all the time the doctor tolde me this morin that this was but littel chance of his recovery his putts was a hundred and sitey and if hely lower quill he would not live live

my wife I do not like to be in the hospitel there so many men in there sick moste of them are o my dear wife I wish you was here he called for you last night last night he called ma three times and it made me fel so bad all the I sit alone in the Hosital I am got to be up all the night againg with my de child or my dear wif I you austin was here my de wife I feel very sad myself if it is the Lords will that I should have to leave my dear whilde in tennsee and come home without him i never conbin
