Charles Pettit Esquire 20th Decr 1803 Bird Wilson respecting costs in Case of George Peter

Page 2

Sir Unless the costs of the commission of bankruptcy against George Peters are paid this day or tommorrow, I shall be obliged, in compliance with directions of the Commissioners, to commence suits for them upon the bond given by you and the Satimis I am, Sir your obt hble Servant Bird Wilson 20th December 1803 Chas Pettit Esqr
