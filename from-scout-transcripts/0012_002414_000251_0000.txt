Dear Sir I have yours by last post, Accompanying My Pendts , which May answer the end desired _ I Now enclose you the oath of Nine and Lawson, with the Certificate of Mr Parviance the Collector, Which will No doubt be Sufficient, to Cancel the Policy of Insurance Made, from Bermuda_ as the property on which the Said Insurance was Made, is still there in Stau quo___ I am in haste Your friend Thomas Truxtun Balt illegible Feb. 7, 1796 S Sterett Esqr
