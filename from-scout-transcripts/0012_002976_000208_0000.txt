D Sir Your friends are somewhat disappointed, that there are no notes or very few from Harlem. We think it of much importance, that operation should be made, and you will much oblige us as well as serve the cause by lending your aid. Tommorow, it is presumed, curiousity may bring a considerable number to love town _ Here us It will be well worth attention to endeavour to give them a right direction. We shall with great pleasure see you at the head of a good column. at the Plough & Harrow at half past eleven ten o'clock Yrs with great regard A. Hamilton New York , April 29 - 89
