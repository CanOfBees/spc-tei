Military Prison Octr 22nd 1864 Dear Sirs I am held in prison on a charge of Treason It is my desire to give bond & security for my appearance at Court, and to prepare for my defence. Coonsent is given by Lt. Shaw for prisoners to consult Counsel at the Marshals office upon application of Counsel to see prisoner. You will confer a favour that will be duly appreciated if either or both of you will see me at the office designated on this day or at your earliest convenience. Your Obl Servant A G Watkins Mipis O.P. Temple & John Baxter
