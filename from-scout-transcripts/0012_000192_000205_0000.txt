Hd. Qrs. 1st Div. Cav. Corps Blains Cross Roads Decr. 21st 1863 Dear Father I am well but have not time to write & have not had time for several days. Will as soon as possible. A man going through in the morning will take this. The Bag has come & I was much pleased with its contents. Much love to all of you Your aff son Watson B Smith St. & A.A.A.G. 1st Div. Cav. Corps
