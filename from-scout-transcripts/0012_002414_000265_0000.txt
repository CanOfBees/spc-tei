Sir, I send herewith a Statement of my Claim on the Insurance Co. of North America for the Capture &Condemnation of the Sloop Sally and her Cargo. The proofs Inspecting the loss have been deposited at the office for some days past. The Amount of the loss is too Considerable not to Create some Annuity in me for aspindy determination of The Directors, but Independent of that Consideration it may be of Consequence to transmit the papers to England on Account of the Appeal, or to be put into the hands of the Minister of the U S__ I shall hope for an illegible Answer. Whether The Co. think themselves all sent abb. for the loss or not
