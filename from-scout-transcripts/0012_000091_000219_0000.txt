Nash May 23/ 65 My Dear Wife Tuesday Just after Dinner I went down town to write you a few lines in hope that they may find you all well as they leave me tolerable well at present through Mercy yesterday afternoon we were taken out on what they call a skirmish Drill they can call it whatever they Please But i call it a hard one it is a Sham fight we were drilled out in the hot sun and the Colonel was mad and cursing at a great rate he was mad at Some because they could not do right so he took and Drilled us all for about two or three hourse I tell you we were prety tired then we were Prety mad at him i tell you know

So last night he issued 2 New Orders the one was not to sit out side of the Barracks and the other to do away with all the Citizen clothes against the Last of this month or else he would have them all destroyed. I am on what they call camp guard to day i am guarding the Quartermaster s store I tell you he has not got much in it a cup full of Sugar a Day of coffee some soap and candles is about all he has Grubbs told me this morning that we would leave here next week if he knowed Grubbs also told me that Wiley and the first and Second Lieutenants was going to volunteer to go to Mexico if i was at home i do not care if they go to Purgatory for my Part Grubbs told me that Wiley told them that So that Shows that they cant succeed in having to stop here I hope that they cant do anything to hinder us anyhow I want you to keep this to yourself I suppose that
