Jas Latimer Esqre Newyork 4th January 1822 Philadelphia, Dr Sir, We have received your favor of 29 Ulto with some letters for Canton which we forward Mr. Jby Astor to sail the 7th. We are your Most obdt. Servt. John Jacob Astor & Sone PS We have just recd. yours of 2nd which will receive due attention - yrs as before J J A &S
