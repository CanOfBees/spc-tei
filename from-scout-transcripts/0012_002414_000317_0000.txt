John C. Smith Esq. President Ins: Co: of North America

Page 2

To the President and Directors of the Insurance Company of North America, Gentlemen, I would beg leave to decline being considered as a candidate for re-election as a member of the Board of Directors. Wishing Success and happiness to you all, and that the institution may enjoy continued properity, I am, Very Respectfully Your obdt. Servt. Dec 29. 1840 John Stille
