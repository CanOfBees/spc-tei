United States Military Railroads, Division of the Mississippi: Office of General Superintendent, Nashville, Tenn., May 20th 1865. P.S. Lincoln Esq Cincinnati Dear Sir: Your favor of the 13th relating to the Trimble Splice Blocks is at hand. I inclose it herewith calling your attention to indorsement of Mr. N.E. Van Sickle. You will proceed to fill the order as soon as practicable. Very Respectfully W.J. Stevens Gen'l Supt.
