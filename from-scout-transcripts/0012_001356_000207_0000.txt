Nashville Aprile the 21 1865 dear Mother I received your kind letter which gave me great pleasure to here that you are all in good health as this leaves me in at present dear Mother I have not herd from father Since I am getting along first rate and I like the place well the reason you did not get my letter the Bridges is all wasld washed away the are all rite now so you may expect to get a letter soon dear Mother I eclose these two picture for you So you can Send them to my ant that them boys that came down I dad not See any of them hank wheeler is working here with me no more at present

NASHVILLEAPR22TENMr. Austin SmithBinghamtonBroomeN.Y.
