12th January 1805 Wm. Cramond to have note for $2500 withdrawing renew'd which was done Charles Pettit Esquire

Page 2

12 Jany 1805. Sir, I shall be about from town , for a few days, & observe that one Monday G Roberts for note for 2500, with my Indorsement falls due on Monday. I presume it is intended to remain in its present form, till the loss on the Lydia shall be satisfactorily ascertained, and not part into Bank but if a renewal be necessary, it shall be allowed to on my return

Page 3

to town, the later and of next week I am respectfully Your most obed Wm. Cramond.
