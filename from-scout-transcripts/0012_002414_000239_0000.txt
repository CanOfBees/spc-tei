Sir Insurance Office Penns.a March 4. 1801 The Directors of this Office have appointed Thomas Fitzsimons & John G Wachsmuth a Committee to confer with the Committee from your board, relative to Insurances to France & if agreeable to meet at this Office tomorrow at 5 O'Clock I am respectfully Your ob. hl. Servt James S. Cox. Prest Charles Pettit Esqr President Insurance Co North America
