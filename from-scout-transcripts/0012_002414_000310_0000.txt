City of Philadelphia On the eleventh day of June A D 1794 Personally appeared before me John Baker one of the Aldermen of The City of Philadelphia Joseph Rittenhouse who in his solemn affirmation doth declare and say "That there was not on board the Sloop Harmony John Mc Cormick master on her return voyage from Savannah in The State of Georgia to Philadelphia in February or March last any property which could have been covered by an Insurance effected in The office of the Insurance Company of North America on the sixteenth day of January last except twenty casks of Rice amounting with charges to Fifty six pound Three shillings and Seven pence current minuy of The State of Georgia reckoning The Dollar at four shillings and eight pence illegible Dollars Affirmed before me Joseph Rittenhouse John Baker
