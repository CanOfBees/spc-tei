Staunton Va Dece 12 de 1871 Dear Col. I retd. from West Virginia a few days since and found your favor of the 5 church . Mr Bukle , our undertaker, says he will reniure the remains of Young Ramsey and inter them here, in a walnut coffin, for $25. A plain marble slabwill cost from $15 to$30., according to the quality of the stone andthe length of the inscription- We have a Confederate burial ground here. Yours truly R.H.Cartell Col. I.T.L. Preston Lexington Va
