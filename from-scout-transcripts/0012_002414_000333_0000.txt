Sir_ Philadelphia Dec 17. 1792 The establishment of an Insurance Company, on an extensive and liberal plan, is obviously one of the greatest aids that could be given to american Commerce, and I trust the Directors of such a Company will think every method of diminishing the dangers of Navigation as deserving, at least, of examination & experiment._ The inclosed memoir has received no other publicity than a reading before the Philosophical Society in this City, and a few copies I had struck off for particular distribution: Its appearance depends on the publication of the 3d Volume of Transaction. In the meantime give me leave to

Page 3

to present one to your Direction, and another to yourself individually. I have the honor to be with great respect & Esteem Sir Your obedient Servant Jon Williams
