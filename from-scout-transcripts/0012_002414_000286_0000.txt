Page 2

Department of State Septr 27, 1805 Gentlemen, I have received your memorial respecting the proceedings at Paris in the case of the ship New Jersey and cargo, in which the Companies you represent are interested as insurers, and I have the honor to inform you, that on the previous application of other parties ( Messrs. Nichlin &Griffith ) the step has been taken which the circumstances seemed to render proper. I have the honor to be Gentlemen Very respectfully your most Obt Servt. James Madison Charles Pettit and James L. Cox, Esquires Philadelphia
