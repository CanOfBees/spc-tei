Corinth, Mississippi April the 3 D 1862 my Dears Wife I tak the opertuney of Riten you A few lines to let you no that I am well at this time and I hope when thes few lines comes to hands that thay mae find you inday the same blesen I Receve you letter from Mr limones he Rote hit to me hit did releve my mind verre much to reseve A letter from you I was begint to think that I was nevr gone to git A letter from you enmore I was sarre to her that you was out of persisson Inday hit pudni Chil me to hear of hit but tri and git long the best you can tel I Can send you som monney I will send hit to you as son as I can git hit tel Mr Dennis to not let you suffer Inday I wust that I cud com home but but thay dnt love no ferlow I drem A bote you evre nite I drempe one night that we was camp clost to Randolph and I thout you and Mrs Uketson had com to see us I thout that I was fixen to go home with you you better beleve that I was A fixen in A herre when I wak I was fold badly. John is well he send his lov to you. Inday I wount you to leve thar ef the yanks comes thar ef can I wount you to go with sum body that is gon to leve thar tel Mr Dennis ef he leves thar to take you with

him ef he lese but I am in hopes thay wont come thar bille Whillar Com to see us he told me that thay was A man told him that he saw Frank hear I cant git A chant to go and see him Inday I want Com home but I Cant Com now and I dont now when I can for thay wont give no furlows I want you to pick up A big resluson and say you will be content tel my times is out I waunt you to rite to wether I must Gone for the war ar not sum time I think I will git A transfur and go to fort piller Fort Pillow thay will giv me A transfer ef I goin fer the war but I dont waunt to gone for the war now I had droth wate A while longer thay ar looken fo A fite A bote ten miles from this plase what is cold mostray we was orde out thar bute we could not git gunes serfisen to fite with and we did not go i expect thay ar fiten thar to Day I can har the cannon wance and while I dont no wether thay will be eney fiten hear ar not I am A frad that thay will pase fort piller I am A frede ef thay do pase thar thay will do A hepe A dammages to the pepele thar Thar is A haps of sixson in our Camps James Farras A member of our Comney was taken seck A Tusday morne and he Dide the next morne at one o clock hit is sompthing like the brane fever ever one

O. Freeman Rite to me as son as you gite this Inday I nevr has reseve but one letter from you yeat
