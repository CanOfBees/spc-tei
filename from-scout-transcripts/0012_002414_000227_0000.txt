Saml. B. Breese to Eben Hazard Feby. 9. 1799 Power to Transfer Stock. __ .

Page 2

Know all Men by these presents that I, Samuel B. Breese of Shrewsburg, in the County of Monmouth and State of New Jersey, have made ordained, constituted, and by these presents do make, ordain and Consitute, and in my place and stead put and dipute Ebenezer Hazard of Philadelphia my true and lawfull attorney for me, and in my name to sell assign and transfer the whole or any part of the stock standing in my name on the books of the president and directors of the Insurance Company of North America, In witness whereof I have hereunto set my hand &seal this 9th day of February. In the year of our Lord one thousand and seven hundred &ninety nine Sealed and delivered in the presence of Elizabeth Breese Abigail Breese Monmouth County State of New Jersey Sam-el B. Breese Be it Known that this Ninth Day of February in the year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and ninety nine personally appeared before me Samuel Breese Esquire one of the Judges of the Superior Court of commopytion and for the County of Monmouth &State of New Jersey Samuel B. Breese. subscribing party to the above power of attorney. who acknowledged he sealed and delivered the same as his voluntary act I deed for the purpose therein Contained. Acknowledged Before me Samuel Breese
