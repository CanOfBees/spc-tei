dockstrut Saturday 28 Sept dear sir I have received note of yesterday & will have the $4500 paid me an accot of the lef. on Ship Telemachus until you shall have more fully if america the paper - I mean it shall be hold a the property of the campy I am respectfully Sir Your Most Ob Sev Peter Blight Charles Pettit Esq Insurance Company of North America
