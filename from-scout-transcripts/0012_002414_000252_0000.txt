President &Directors of the Insurance offices of North America Letter John Wilcocks dated May 31, 1797

Page 2

To the President &Directors of the Insurance office of North America Gentlemen The premium of Drs. 3501 due this day for Insurance effected at your office has never yet been received from the Gentlemen on whose account the business was transacted, having received the order I unriduid that if I did not execute it I should be liable in case of a loss. I however shall make provision for the debt shortly and must by the favour of your saving the notes from being protested by withdrawing them from the Bank I am very Respectfully Gentlemen Your Obt Servt. John Wilcocks 31 May 797
