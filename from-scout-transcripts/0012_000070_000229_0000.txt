Balt Febry 16, 1861 O P Temple Esq Knoxville Tenn Dear Sir Your favor of the 12th inst is to hand. Please find enclosed Bill of Sugar forwarded, which we hope will be satisfactory. We congratulate you on your State going so strong for the Union, & we are with you here, We will be happy to serve you, Yours truly Wilson & Burns
