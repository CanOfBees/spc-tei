Huntingdon, Tenn. Oct 26" 1911 Miss. Susie Gentry, Franklin, Tennessee, Dear Miss. Gentry; Noticing in the Banner that you are trying to locate the graves of the soldiers of the War of 1812 buried in this State. My Grand-Father Thompson Enochs, who was buried near Westport in the 24" Civil district of this County, was a soldier in the War of 1812. Yours very truly, Wilson Enochs
