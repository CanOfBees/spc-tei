To the President &Directors of the North America Insurance Company Philadelphia. Novem 24th 1806 Joseph Reed & Reinhard Peters, application for $10000 on Respindentia & Ship Bingham granted

Page 2

To the President and Directors of the North America Insurance Company Gentlemen We wish to borrow on Respondentia ten thousand Dollars ($10,000) on the Ship Bingham from Philadelphia to Calcutta and back to Philadelphia The securities we offer are Andrew Bayard and Josph Cwriuen . Will you please inform us at what rate per cent, and where this sum can be obtained. lor all very Respectfully your obt. servt. Jos: Reed Richd. Petres illegible Phila. Nov 24th 1806
