Camden 23 rd Dec 1781 My Dear White I have to request you vow to push with celerity to this place, afield of Glory will probably present a little lower down the country of which I wish you to participate I shall wait for you until Tuesday on Christmas morning till when & were believe your Affectionate friend & humble Svt Anthony Wayne Lieut Col White
