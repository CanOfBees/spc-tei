October 10. 1806 Thomas P. Cope application for loan on respondentia agreed_

Page 2

Gentlemen I understand that you have money to let on respondentia _ I want $20.000 for the new Ship Surquchanna Cap. Blackiston for Calcutta_ provided the terms can be rendered mutually satisfactory_ I offer you as my sureties Gn. K. Helmuth & Henry K Helmuth_ Yours Sr Thos. P Cope Octor. 10th 1806 The President & Directors of the N. America Insurance Office
