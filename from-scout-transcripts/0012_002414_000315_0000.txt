Philada. July 17. 1833. Dear Sir, My services in relation to the cases before the Commissioners under the treaty with France, known by the name of the Antwerp cases, being ended, I beg leave to enclose to you a bill, which I hope may be found right, Yrs. truly John Sergeant John C Smith Esq. Prest. N A Insce. Company
