Knoxville Tenn. Nov. 16th 8 o'c PM Dear Father We are here all right excepting some small lopes - the rebs. made us "get back"- I am all right - the nearest I came to being shot was a bullet in my coat collar. Dont worry about me. I am well & in good spirits. With much love to all of you Your son Watson
