Camp Near Nashville Tenn Dec 8th 1862 Dear Mother I took my pen in hand to rite you a few lines to let you know that we are all well at presant and hope that you are the same. the boys are a having a good time now sence thay were payde off we were payde of for four months the same day that the

Page 2

Col started home we are a going to payde the first of January for the rest. I wount have mutch to send home this time Alexander mongomery and John Bresler was here to day. there were nine new recruts come in our company to day from Butler Co I hafft to stop riting to day for I cant think of anything new I will rite more some other day we are still at Nashville Southgate Cregmile Nashville Tenn Sunday Morning
