Hills Jany 7th, 1798 Sir The Board of Managers having called on me for the payment of interest due, and as I am seperated from my Papers I will be obliged if you will send me an acct thereof that I may take immediate measures to raise the Money, or a good Note for the amot &if the latter may tell me at what sight you think it may be admitted ( paying the disct.) I am Sir Your Obed Servt Rob Morris Mr. Ebenr. Hazard
