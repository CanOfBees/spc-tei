The State of Geo Gilmore County August the twenth mis Leathey Gene mincy after my complements i can say to you i am better then i was when i saw you and i truly hope those lines may come cufe to hand and find you well i was fore days a giten home after i left Loudon i will be back in afew Days if i mend on i woud be glad to see you i am not rite musafe at this time i will try to stop as i go back but i wont you to rite as soon as you git this leter and let me know how you comeon i wosh i cood rite more but i can not at this time rite Direct your leters to white path
