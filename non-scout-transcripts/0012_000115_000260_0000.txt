Agreement Robert Allen & David Burford

Page 2

This Article mad this 22 day of November in the year of our Lord 1819 between Robert Allen and David Burford that for the purposes of carrying on Trade and merchandise the said parties have entered into Partners hope on the following Terms the said Robert Allen is to furnish four thousand dollars capital and a credit in Philadelphia sufficient to complete an aportment of goods suitable to bind in carthage and the said David Burford is to furnish two thousand Dollars capital on his part and to transit and carry on the business entirely and each party to receive and be entitled to an Equal share of the prophits arising from said business at the expiration of the partnership which is understood to be four years from the date here of it is understood that the expenses necessary in carrying on the business is to be mutual and equal such as house rent store keeper time & board Traveling expense fire wood to and to be taken out of the prophits but that the individual expence of Either party as well as their clothing and every thing that is not in incured necessaryly in Carrying on the business is at the private expense of the party incurring it and to be charged to him and taken out of his share of the Profits a true and

complete ant is to be kept thereof and charged on the books open to the inspection of all parties the sum furnished by the said parties first by each to be drawn out of the business at the disolution the residul of the prophets to be Equaly divided after all debts paid in such a way as shall be fair and consistant the the nature of the business - the said David Burford is not to Enter into any Trade or speculation but what is for the benefit of the firm during the time of partnership not to enter security or become liable for others contracts without the consint of the said Robert Allen the business to be carried on under the firm of R Allen & Burford in Testimony Whereof we have herewith set our hands & seals the Day & year first Written Robert Allen David Burford Duplicates hereof signed
