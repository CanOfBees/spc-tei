2 Aug. 15 61 Dear wife I shall not be at home as soon as I expected. I will not be home for some two week I am well and hope these lines may find you are the same we have very lasy times here at camp I would like to see you all

Page 2

I will be at home two weeks from tomorrow if I keep well we exspect our uniforms this week I have bought my self I started a letter home last sun day & as George Gavers was coming home i thought i would send you a few to let you how i was

Page 3

I cannot tell where we will go from this place No more at present but remain your affectionate Husband Henry Brown i would like to have a quart of good cane molasses
