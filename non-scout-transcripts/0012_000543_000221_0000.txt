Sir, Authorized by your letter of the 15th May last to draw on you at sight for Five hundred Dollars, appropriated for the promotion of Indian civilization. I have drawn for that sum in favor of Josiah Nichol this day. I am Sir yr Obedient Servant Return J. Meigs.
