To Isaiah and george Now boys from the way you all write I think you are doing verry well i hope you will continue So untill get home and You will See things That you never saw Something good to eat And drink Boath for You and your mother you must watch the Squirrels out of your Corn field when it Begins to come up And tend it the best you can for the Roots i think you Can raise Some good In that new ground

If it is well tended And a good season Now be right good Boys and do your work well what you do I would like to know Whether your bread Stuff was agoing to Last you till after Harvest or not No more at present But Still remain Affectionate father untill Death Be good boys To Isaiah and george Brown From their father Henry Brown
