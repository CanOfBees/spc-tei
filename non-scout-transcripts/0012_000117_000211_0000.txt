"Mail" New Orleans August 16th 1853 David Burford Esq Dixon Springs, Smith Co, Tenn, Dear Sir, Your account has Credit Five Thousand Dollars $5.000 - for W. B. Shaphard Chk for that amount received in July of 10th July Just at hand. We note Your mistrusting about investing that amount @ 8% for undoubted Security. On 4 Nov time . Our Mr. Perkins will write you further on this subject when well - He is now sick Very Respy Perkins, Campbell & Co for P. L. Kennen
