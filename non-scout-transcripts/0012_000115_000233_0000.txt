Page 2

Alexand DC Nov. 15th 1831 Dear Burford I this day make a shipt of 13 likely negros, 8 men & 5 girls no. 1. If they should reach the market before the passage of the interdiction they will ouub Hip pay a handsome profit - Our subsequent coun will depend upon the advise we receive from W. Franklin - Let me hear from you & give me the news at long Y. Friend JM Saunder The whole number up to this time are 42
