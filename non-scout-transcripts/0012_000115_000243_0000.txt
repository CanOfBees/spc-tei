Colonel D. Burford Smith County Tenn Handed by Mr William Patterson From Tilmon Bethell up for Door Keeper to the Senate

Page 2

Tennessee Smith County Sept the 1st 1831 Dear Sir I wish to inform you that I expect to be a candidate for door keeper & wish your support I have not the oportunity to come & see you I would be glad to receive a line from you: I expect you have had several such appreciations I sent by a friend down to cort but he tell me you ware not there if any man in Smith county Needy the place worse than I do: I dont know him I am your respectfully Tilmon Bethell
