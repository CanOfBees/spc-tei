William Trimble Vs William Hall Writ in Debt To August 1784 Fr. 16th July 1784 Executed by me to jepe LeeroyTaylor Bail

Page 2

State of North Carolina To the Sheriff of Washington Co We command you to take William Hall planter if in your Bailwick and him Safely keep so that you have him before the Justices of our County Court of pleas and quarter Sessions to be Held on the first Monday in August next then and there to answer William Trimble in a plea of debt damage fifty pounds Specie Herein fail not and Have you there this Writ. Witness John Sevier Clerke of our Said Court at office the first monday in May Anno Domini 1784 John Sevier C.C.
