The next letter I shall write to our Son James tell him that he must get Andrew to write a few lines in it also charge them to be good Boys so as no complaints comes to the house about them it would not give you much trouble make that Practice writing I am astonished to see so many here that cant write a letter home to there wives it is strange when they can pick up all manner of mischief that they cant pick that of writing up I wish you would put them tools away them saws what hangs in the Kitchen and Rub a little oil out of that tin lamp on a rag and grease them over them 2 canes up the Gavret and Some Paper on the Gavret floor Put in that big Box i did not take time to put anything away I shall close no I dont know of anything else that I want to state

I saw that Oliver this morning he saw that he would not write for a few Days I saw John Straight Georges Brother I shall write Robert a Letter tomorrow if nothing happens this is a nice day Sun Shine but it is muddy under write me a letter we may be here for a while we cant tell as yet all of our fellows are not out yet So good night it is pretty hard to sit down on the floor of our tent and write with the Portfolio on your lap that is a specimen of camp life so any high minded man is brought down a little here Kiss all the children for me. There is a whole crew of women here I dont know what business decent women has here Direct Camp Curtin Harrisburgh Pa Care of Cap Wiley Give my respects to Mr Holmes Your Husband David
