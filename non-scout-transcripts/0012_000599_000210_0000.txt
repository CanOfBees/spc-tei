Camp Clark September 20th 1861 Dear wife i thoug I i would drop a few lines to in form you that i am still a live we left Camp Chase on Tuesday Evening and got to Cincinnati the next morning About day light and we staid there two days and then left for this place we got here on Saturday after noon at about 4 oclock we had to Pay our way at that and it strapt me clear i want you to get one dollar and send it to me if you can I think you can get it from Lybrean Maxwell if he has got it by him I cant
