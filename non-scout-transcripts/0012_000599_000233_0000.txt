Camp of many soldiers 33 miles below Nashville South March 25th 1862 Caroline M. Brown Dear wife it is with much love I sit down to let you know that i am well At this time and i do hope these few lines will find you And the boys injoying the same Blessing now i feel verry sorry that i cant get any word from you i have waited and look for A letter but none come i have Not had but one letter from you since i got back to camp And this is the Eleventh one that i have wrote to you since I got back i begin to feel the you have forsaken me
