FT. SNELLING, MINNEAPOLIS, MINN. The Ft. Snelling reservation comprises about 2,000 acres, and is located midway between Minneapolis and St. Paul. It was established at a military post by the U. S. government in 1819 and maintains the largest garrison in the northwest. Salt Lake City Aug. 3, 1921 Dear Ralph, I have traveled through a good many states this summer as well as Canada. I hope you too are enjoying the vacation. Your friend, Oha Maks Mr. Ralph Callahan 68 Hall St. Asheville N. Car.
