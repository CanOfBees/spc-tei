Memo for Wm G. Burford you will call at the State Bank of Tn and draw the amount of draft which is $6,905 30/100 and deposit in Planters Bank $5000 and bring home $1905.30 and on your return go and pay all our notes which are due and bring them to me together with the balance of the money on hand. January 3rd 1854 D. Burford Note direct the Cashr Planters Bank to give you a Bank Book with the entry of deposit made in it. Also leave with Wm B. Sheperd & Co the land warrant to whom it belongs - they are on the Square
