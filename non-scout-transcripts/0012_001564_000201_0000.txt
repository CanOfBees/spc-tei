Knoxville Tn Dec. 11 1863 Dear Wife I Seat my Self the Second time to write you a few lins, to let you know where I am and how I am. I am now at Knoxville and have bin here 20 days We have bin fighting the Enomy all the time we have bin here. They have made for or five Eferts to take Knoxville but they have failed every time with a grate loss. the made a charge the other morning and we killed 400 hundred and taken 500 prisners we, lost, about fifty wounded, and, killed I expect you have heard that we have bin taken a dozen times but It is a lie we ar not and Knoxville will be dranched with blood before

It is taken It is all write I think the News came this morning that Sherman is in ten miles of this with a large force the Rebels is moving up the country I expect you can hear the canons roaring and I no it makes your heart ache. We have not bin ingaged much we belong to the rite wing of this army they have bin fighting on the left wing we have had some fighting but not like they have at the lower end of the Town the last fight they had they said the blood ran in the Entrechments. I have bin well all the time with the Exceptions of the same Eys they have bin very bad but they ar a little better I have never heard from

Father and the boys I expect they are at the Cumberlan gap Em I am a frade I cant come home when I promised you I would tha very time taht I wanted to come If I dont get to com you must do the best you can Em If my Eyes dont get better I will have to leave the army and come home. I think I ot to be at home any how I now I want to be at home with my beloved little familey It is all I Study about day and night I will have to bring my few lins to a close I dont no whether you can read it or not My fingers is very near frose I have to write on my nea
