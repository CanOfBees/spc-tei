Washington April 15th 1820 Dear Sir I send you the Enclosed letter to put you on your guard from running into the same Error or putting yourself in the power of these Swindlers Sell at Orleans if you get but two cents pr pound - I have no hopes that any thing I can say will be attend to by Mr. Chester who seems bent on destruction you will notice his people priced my tobacco at 8 cents & took commissory & accordingly by now it nets me about three cents I cannot think or talk of my cursed unfortunate businesses which is to Ruin me without my Judgement or wishes consulted in most afet your friend Robert Allen
