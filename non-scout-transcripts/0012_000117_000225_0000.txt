Col Burford Dixon Springs Smith Cty Wrote Thos Gordrum 21 March 1833

Page 2

Nashville March 18 1833 Dear Sir I am this fan on my way to New Orleans and have directed my overseer Thos. Goodrum not to put your mane to Lockholden until he arrived a letter from you. Write to him on this day ick and he will attend to it. Direct to Mr Pleassumt This Bad is about to attend and Light only time to pay if head up Jo am L.L.B loodrd
