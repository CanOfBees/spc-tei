February 23th 1862 Dear wife I take this time to in form you that I am well at this time all but a But a bad cold I have had three spells of coffin the 3 last nights I do cincearly wish that these few lines will Find you injoying the same blessing I received your kind letter and was glad to hear from you and here that you wore well at that time and the rest of the family I feel truly glad that you were doingn so well and kept your health so well I was verry glad to hear that your feed held out so well as it does I was glad to hear that the boys helpt you so well I do hope they

Page 2

will still continue to help you Now i will tell you whare we are campt at this time we are campt Near Bards Town by 29 miles below Lebannon 39 miles from Louis ville and about the distance from mumfordsvill the place we are now is a verry Nice Country some verry good Houses through here and slaves plenty as for whare we will go from here we do not know and we do not care much some of the as well as myself thinks if they ware foot loos They would stay so the cause is good anough but it is the way things are caried on in this war by the officers especely in this regment

Page 3

This is the 6th letter i have wrote to you since i got back to camp and only one from I want you write once a week As long i remain in the service and i will try to send ONe every week to you uif i can raise the stamps and paper we have got good From others places they say they have Buckner and all his supplise and 50 peices of cannon we here a grate deal of news here we do not know whether it is tru or not the last letter i rote i put you A good pipe stem in it for you to put in your pipe and smoke once every day untill get back if so lucky as to return so good

By i want you to direct you letters to Bards town Was Nelson Co Ky No more at present But still remain your tru husband untill Death Henry Brown Now i have to quit and Clean my gun for drill Now boys be good to your mother i was verry glad that Isaiah thought anough of me to say a few words to me by letter now Georg i would lik to see you And have a chat with you Good by Boys write soon Henry Brown
