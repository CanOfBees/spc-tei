Camp Near Tuscumbia Alabama August First 62 CAROLINE M. BROWN Dear wife, I. Sit down To inform you whare I am at this time i am In the old camp our regt Left last mondy aweek Ago for tennessee but There is 35 of the 31st Here that was Sick and Lame together we Exspect To leave to morrow we Get no letter here any More they go on to the Regt we have bin here Better than a month And i have only got one

Page 2

Letter since we have bin here And i think the time very Long i have wrote but One for i felt so bad I could not think of Writeing but now i am Better i thought i would write a few lines to let you know how i am I feel better the Sweling Has left my feet and legs Pretty near but they are very week and sore yet Robert Conn is Sitting By my Side Eating his Breakfast he is all rite I hope these few lines will Find you and the rest of The family all well and Doing the best you can For your Selves it is not worth while Say any

Page 3

Thing about the war for i Know nothing to say only It Still goes on down here we Know that much about it As for comeing home it It is a very dul chance I think at the present Time we have our own fun with the Nigroes when They come in camp to sell Fish and Eggs and chickens And pies corn bread and Such likes i went this Morning to get Some butter And they ask me 40 cts Per pound and i told them I would See their house Burn first So i went back To camp with out it i have Paid at the rate of 75 cts per Dozen and other things in Propotion our Sutters Sells

Page 4

Salt at 15 Dollars a barrel Flour at twenty Dollars We have to pay for Every Thing we buy down here I tell you i can not Eat The crackers since i have been Sick we can buy bread At a dear rate what you would give a man for a Pice for 10 cents it is What we would call a Light cake at home and Small at that but verry Good bread so you may Guess what we have To pay for our grub Here now i shall have to Close by saying that i would like to come home To See you all once more So good to you all for this Time write Soon HENRY BROWN.
