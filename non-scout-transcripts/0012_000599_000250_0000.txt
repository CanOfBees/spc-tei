Like to have Some of your young Chickens to eat We have to pay 25 cents For a chickens about Half grown and 2 Dollars A bushels for potatoes And 30 cts for butter And 25 cts for one Dozen of Eggs and Every Thing in porpotion Now i shall Close My Scribling to you For this time by saying I hope these few lines May find you all in Good health My love to You all and requiring Friends good by to you All Henry Brown

A few lines to the boys Well boys we are A grate ways apart at This time and we do Not know how long We will be apart I Hope not long we are Now in alabama whare I did not think I would Be here when I was at Home with you last Winter I thought I would bin at home Long be fore this time But it is hard to tell when we will be at Home now but my Prare is that you Will be good boys

I will tell you that I fount a revolver that I was offered 12 Dollars For it the Same day that I found it but I told The boys that money would not buy it if I get home before Winter we will have Some fun Shooting Rabbits and I have Picked out one of the Best Drums in Servise That i bve be discharged Fetch home with me For you boys to Now I Shall close be good Boys my love to you boath Henry Brown to the Boys
