William Trimble Vs James Ashmore & Hezekiah Ashmore Writ in lease To August 1783 Fr. 22 July 1783

Page 2

State of North Carolina To the Sheriff of Washington Co We command you to take James Ashmore and Hezekiah Ashmore (planters) if to be found in your Bailwick and them safely keep so that you have them before the Justices of our County Court of pleas and Quarter Sessions to be Held on the first Monday in August next then & there to answer William Trimble in a plea on the case of damage one Thousand pounds Specie, Herein fail not and Have you there this Writ. Witness John Sevier Clerke of our Said Court at his office, The first Monday in May anno Domini 1783 J Sevier CC
