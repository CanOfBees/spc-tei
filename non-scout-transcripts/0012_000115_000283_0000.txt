Mr. Joseph Allen Mr Meary Dear Sir I regret to have to apprise you of the death of your brother Cnl Allen on this morning about 1 a.m. He was quite well all day yesterday & retired yesterday morning about 8 o''clock apparently in good health, was sleeping genelty about half past twelve and in his servants going to him about one o''clock was found quite cold. He seemed to have died without a struggle. His funeral will take place tomorrow at 4 P.M. in order to give you & his & M Joseph an opportunity to be present His servant John takes this to you & you will be pleased to have him return with you tomorrow. With much respect Winchester Neale
