Matthew Tolbert V. Samuel Davis AC. in Case to November 1782 from 6th Sep 1782 W.C.P.P.

Page 2

State of No Carolina To the Sheriff of Washington Co We command you to take Samuel Davis - if in your bailwick and him safely keep so that you have him before our court of pleas and quarter sessions to be held on the first monday in November next then and there to answer Matthew Tolbert in a plea of tresspass on the lease Damage five Hundred Thousand pounds Herein fail not and have you there this writ witness John Sevier Clerk of our Said Court at office the first monday in August. anno Domini 1782 &in the Seventh year of our Independence W. Cocke att P.P. John Sevier C.W.C.
