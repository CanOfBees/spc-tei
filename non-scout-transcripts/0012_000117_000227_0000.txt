D Burford Esq. Dear Sir I enclose herewith a certificate of Deposit from the Un. Bk. no 9 for one hundred eighty three 44/100 Dolls the amt. of funds you gave me for that purpose. I had some difficulty in getting it payable with interest, they say they do not like issue them in small amounts. Your friend John Jones Nashville Apl 28 1838
