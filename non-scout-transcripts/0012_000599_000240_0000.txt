Camp near Corrinth Mississippi May 19th 62 Caroline M. Brown Dear wife and children I seat my self this nice maining to respond to yours of the 22 of last month and the 7 of this i got one last saturday And one yesturday i was truly glad to hear from you A gain and that you ware well then I cant brag of my health at this time it has bin verry poor for some timei have had the direa ever since the next day After Holden was to see us i am able to be about

yet and i thank god for it I hope i shall soon be well again we are within 2 miles of the rebels we can hear their drums plain i think the fight will go off soon I do not exspect to be in it i am not able to fight at this time this will end the war if we gain it And we have men enough to do it the bigest days work that ever i saw was done yesterday diging rifles pitts and throwing brest works and planting cannon it looks pretty hard to see so many men at work on sunday to mee i tell you and that fixing to kill one another
