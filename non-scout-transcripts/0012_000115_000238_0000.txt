D. Burford Esq Dixon Springs Tennessee From Maberry, Giller, & Watkins - recd, 11th March 1839

Page 2

Philad, February 25th 1839 Dear Sir, Yours of the 10th Jan we have rec c Stating you had received from W Thomas Pheps $250, last March & had written us requesting us to draw on you for that amount - Which letter we have never received - we believe there is no bank nearer you than Nashville - if it is not troubling you too much, we shall be be obliged if you will send us a draft for the amount please enclose their not to us also - Respectfully Maberry, Giller & Watkin You will please accept our thanks for your attention - M G & W D. Burford Esq
